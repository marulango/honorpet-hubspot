Services We Offer

You've given your pet a lifetime of love. Now, create a farewell that honors your special bond. As your guide, we provide the time, space, and options you need to say goodbye in your own way—all in one place.

Euthanasia

Your pet deserves a gentle goodbye. In our calm and home-like setting, our caring and experienced team will support you and your pet through a dignified transition, providing the time and space you need.

Learn More

Water Cremation

Choose an eco-friendly farewell that honors your pet and our planet. This gentle process uses water and minerals, producing 90% less carbon emissions than traditional methods. Choosing this option shows your commitment to a sustainable future.

Learn More

Pre-Planning

Gain peace of mind with thoughtful preparation. Whether planning ahead or needing immediate support, we'll guide you through quality of life considerations and end-of-life decisions that align with your wishes for your best friend.

Learn More

Pet Loss Support

Your grief journey is personal. Connect with our licensed social worker through no-cost virtual support groups, one-on-one sessions, or creative workshops focused on remembrance, healing, and community.

Your Goodbye, Your Way

Your story with your pet is one-of-a-kind, and we're your partner in creating a farewell that honors your special bond. From peaceful transitions to meaningful memorials, we guide you through options you might not know existed. You'll have the choices and support you need to make these moments truly your own.

Always Connected

From first hello to final farewell, your pet never leaves our care. With all services under one roof and our team personally guiding you every step of the way, you'll always know who's caring for your pet and what comes next. No handoffs, no uncertainty—just the peace of mind of consistent, compassionate care.

Gentle on Hearts, Gentle on Earth

Caring for the world your pet loved is part of honoring their memory. Our commitment to sustainability extends beyond water cremation to every aspect of our care. From our Comfort Center built with reclaimed materials to our all-electric fleet and eco-conscious memorial options, we've designed an experience where honoring your pet can also honor the planet.

In good hands

Our Chief Veterinary Officer, Dr. Shea Cox, is a pioneer in veterinary hospice and end-of-life care. With over a decade dedicated to advancing and innovating how we say goodbye, Dr. Cox brings unmatched expertise, compassion, and understanding to every interaction. Trust in her guidance and commitment to helping you and your pet navigate this transition with confidence and care.
